Welcome to EduTransform!

EduTransform is a cutting-edge educational platform dedicated to revolutionizing the way we learn and teach. With innovative features and advanced technologies, we empower educators and learners to achieve their full potential.

What We Do

AI Assignments

AI Assignments is a feature that automatically generates assignments based on lesson PDFs using artificial intelligence algorithms. Teachers can upload lesson materials in PDF format, and the system analyzes the content to create customized assignments tailored to the lesson topics.

Emotion based feedback

Emotion based feedback is a feature that enables teachers to provide feedback to students based on their emotional state. Using sentiment analysis techniques, the system analyzes student responses and provides personalized feedback tailored to their emotions.

TimeTable Generation

TimeTable Generation is a feature designed to automate the process of creating class schedules. It takes into account various constraints such as teacher availability, classroom availability, and student preferences to generate optimized timetables for schools or educational institutions.

Student paced worksheets

Student paced worksheets is a feature that allows students to progress through educational materials at their own pace. Teachers can create interactive worksheets with self-assessment tools, enabling students to learn and practice topics independently while receiving immediate feedback.
